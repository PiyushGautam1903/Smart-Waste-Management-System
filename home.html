About Us

At Smart Waste Management, we are committed to revolutionizing waste management with smart and efficient solutions. Our mission is to improve waste collection, reduce pollution, and promote sustainable practices through technology.

Our project, Waste Tracking and Optimization Platform, focuses on monitoring waste levels in bins using IoT-powered IR sensors. When a bin reaches full capacity, our system sends an instant pop-up notification to ensure timely collection. The collected sensor data is stored in MySQL for tracking and analysis.

Additionally, we fetch live waste data from online sources to visualize waste generation across different states. Our interactive dashboard, built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, features a dark mode theme, smooth animations, and real-time pie charts to highlight the most polluted areas.

With our innovative approach, we aim to create cleaner cities and smarter waste management systems. Join us in making a sustainable impact for a greener future.
